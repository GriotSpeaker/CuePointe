Our Story

BORN ON THE
STREETS OF
KABALAGALA

In Uganda, pool table sports have long been a favorite pastime — especially among urban youth. Yet for too long, the sport remained informal, with few avenues for growth, recognition, or career development.

CuePointe was founded in the bustling neighborhoods of Kabalagala, Kansanga, and along Ggaba Road, driven by one belief: every player deserves a platform to shine.
